About Gaia

Gaia is an intelligent, sustainable ecosystem created for the needs of the modern farmer, combining advanced hardware sensors with powerful artificial intelligence to optimize every aspect of plant care. What truly sets Gaia apart is its innovative approach to sustainability. It repurposes recycled water generated from cooling data centers and redirects it for irrigation.

With Gaia, farming becomes more efficient, cost-effective, and environmentally responsible. It empowers farmers to care not only for their crops, but also for the planet—creating a balance between technological progress and ecological preservation.
